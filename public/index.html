👾 WHO IS BENJAMIN FILLER?

I'm a media creator, director, and narrative systems designer from Michigan. I tell stories across film, games, and interactive spaces.

My work blends cinematic technique, code-based immersion, and analog grit. I like strange archives, gripping installations, damaged footage, modular synths, and custom hardware.
